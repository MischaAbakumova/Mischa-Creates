Cookie Monster is an interactive game developed in Open Framworks. It uses Face Track library by Kyle McDonald to enable you become the caracter. The objective of the game is to use your mouth to eat as much as you can. But be careful! Not everything is good for you.

This is an educational game that aims to teach youngster the importance of a balance diet. Nowadays many types of food are stigmatized for being unhealthy and dangerous. GMO, fast food, pesticides created the culture of the fear. This game teaches that if you are conscious about your eating habits and balance fatty food with vegetables and exercise, you will be OK.

Playtests

The mechanics of the game allows the player to use his or her face to control the character. The goal of the game is to eat everything of the screen, but remain healthy. It could be tricky, as some food items will make you fat and slow and some will make you lose weight.

The online version is coming up soon.
