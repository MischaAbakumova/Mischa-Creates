Inkling

Business cards made easier

We've all been there, right? Sieving through the pile of cards to find out that the one you are looking for is nowhere to be seen. Inkling is an app that solves the problem of storing and retrieving business cards easily and efficiently. It uses an OCR technology to digitize cards to which you attach your inklings for intuitive retrieval at a later time, on your device, on-the-go.
