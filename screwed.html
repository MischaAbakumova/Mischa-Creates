    Screwed

    Screwed is a sculptural data visualisation that explores tense relationship between humans and the Planet by looking at various alterations that our home has been sustaining. Years of urbanization, excavation, deforestation, overfishing, accumulating air and plastic pollution led to severe deformation of the environment that would take centuries, if not millinia to overcome. In some areas the demage is inreversable.
    The sculpture examines the relationship between different areas, the severity of the human impact and the amount of time it would take for the region to recover if the human kind were to disapper.
