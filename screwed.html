    Screwed

    Screwed is a sculptural installation that explores relationship between humans and the Planet. exploring the consequences of human impact on the planet. Every day we fill our car tanks with gasoline, warm our houses with coal and natural gas, cut trees to build roads and farms. But how often do we ask where these goods come from and at what price? The price is centuries of destruction, urbanization, excavation, deforestation and accumulating pollution. How long would it take for the Earth to recover from our malicious actions? Months, decades, centuries? Would the Earth ever be able to come back to its original conditions? "Screwed" explores the time it would take for different regions and areas to recover if human kind were to disappear. Every screw is proportionate to the level of modification and the time period. Symbolically, when I was working on the piece, the globe started to cripple unable to sustained the impact. Have we gone to the point of no return? Is there no hope?
